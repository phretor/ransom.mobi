Lockerpin

This ransomware family is one of the most recent for Android platform. It obtain Device Administrator rights in a underhand manner, by superimposing a fake update screen on the original system view. The user cannot do anything but accept the "update", granting such permissions to the malware.

This kind of ransomwares usually does not encrypt files, but locks the device by changing the unlock pin with a new one that is generated randomly.

A ransom request is shown through a fake FBI message.
