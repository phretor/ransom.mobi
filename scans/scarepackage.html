Scarepackage

Members of this family usually impersonate some well-know apps, usually a Flash player or an anti-virus, and lock the device by preventing the user to do anything with the device.

Moreover the malware takes a picture from the front facing camera (that usually frames the user's face) and shows it together with the ransom request (about $300-$500) to scary the user.
